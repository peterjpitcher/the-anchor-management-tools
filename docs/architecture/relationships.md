## Auth & RBAC

- **Session guard**: `src/app/(authenticated)/layout.tsx` → `getUser()`; redirects unauthenticated → `/auth/login`, no-permission → `/portal/shifts`. See [[overview#Auth Model]].

- **Permission loader / checker**: [[server-actions#rbac.ts|rbac.ts]] (`getUserPermissions`, `checkUserPermission`); seeded into client `PermissionContext`.
- **Cron auth**: `authorizeCronRequest` (`src/lib/cron-auth.ts`) — all [[routes#Cron|cron routes]].
- **API-key auth**: `withApiAuth` (`src/lib/api/auth.ts`) — `/api/events*`, `/api/table-bookings`, `/api/public/private-booking`, `/api/private-booking-enquiry`, `/api/recruitment/applications`.
