## Integration hubs

### [[twilio]]
SMS send/receive. Webhook at `/api/webhooks/twilio`. Used by: bookings, parking, events, messages, private-bookings. Env: `TWILIO_*`, `SMS_SAFETY_*` guards.

### [[microsoft-graph]]
Outlook email send. Used by: invoices, quotes, employees, rota, OJ projects, private bookings. Env: `MICROSOFT_*`, `DOCUMENT_EMAIL_SENDER`, `MANAGER_EMAIL`, `PAYROLL_ACCOUNTANT_EMAIL`.

### [[paypal]]
Deposits and payments. Multiple webhook variants ([[webhooks/paypal/parking]], [[webhooks/paypal/private-bookings]], [[webhooks/paypal/table-bookings]], plus root). Env: `PAYPAL_*`.

### [[stripe]]
Event payments (legacy). Webhook at `/api/stripe/webhook`. Env: `STRIPE_SECRET_KEY`, `STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET`.

### [[openai]]
Receipt classification, event content/SEO, menu parsing. Env: `OPENAI_API_KEY`, `OPENAI_BASE_URL`, `OPENAI_RECEIPTS_MODEL`, `OPENAI_EVENT_CONTENT_MODEL`, `OPENAI_EVENT_SEO_MODEL`, `OPENAI_EVENTS_MODEL`.

### [[googleapis]] (Google Calendar + Routes API)
Rota calendar sync, interview calendar, mileage distance lookups. Env: `GOOGLE_CALENDAR_*`, `GOOGLE_CLIENT_*`, `GOOGLE_REFRESH_TOKEN`, `GOOGLE_SERVICE_ACCOUNT_KEY`, `GOOGLE_ROUTES_API_KEY`.

### [[turnstile]] (Cloudflare)
Bot protection for public booking endpoints. Env: `TURNSTILE_SECRET_KEY`.

### [[github]]
Bug reports filed via `/api/bug-report`. Env: `GITHUB_BUG_REPORTER_TOKEN`, `GITHUB_OWNER`, `GITHUB_REPO`.
